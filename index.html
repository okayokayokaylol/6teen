You Ask

We Answer

Everything you need to know about 6teen products.

What's in a 6teen disposable? +
Depending on the product: premium distillate, live resin, or liquid diamonds. All sourced from licensed California cultivators. No additives, no cutting agents — just cannabis oil and cannabis-derived terpenes.
Are 6teen products lab tested? +
Every batch goes through third-party lab testing for potency, pesticides, residual solvents, and heavy metals. COAs available on request.
Where can I buy 6teen? +
Available at licensed California dispensaries. Use the "Find Near Me" button to locate a retailer close to you.
Can my dispensary carry 6teen? +
Yes. We work with licensed California retailers. Reach out through the contact page and our distribution team will get back to you.
Do the 2g pens need charging? +
Yes — the Purple Reign 2g comes with a rechargeable battery and USB-C port. The 1g units are fully disposable, no charging needed.
